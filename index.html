Sarah's SUP Blog:

12/29/2014: Did you know??? Sarah is paddling a new 12'6" X 25.5" Starboard "Race" SUP now. Her 12'6" 2014 Starboard AllStar is for sale at Urban Surf if you want to buy it used for $2000, OBO. The "Race" has much less rocker than the "Allstar," and will be much more challenging to paddle in anything but flat water. Sarah says she doesn't care whether or not the 2014 "Allstar" sells or not because she will still need that board for choppy times and for downwinding. In the meantime, Sarah can use her Naish "One" iSUP (inflatable paddleboard) for SUPing any condition.
